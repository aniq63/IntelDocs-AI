Less time spent hunting for the right file. More time deciding.

// Features

Everything a company needs to turn documents into answers

One workspace per company, unlimited teams underneath it, and a chat interface that always cites where its answer came from.

Company & team workspaces

Register your company once, then create as many teams as you need — each with its own login, its own documents, and its own chat history.

A private knowledge base

Upload documents at the company level or inside a specific team. Everything is versioned, replaceable, and easy to find by name.

Context-aware chat

Ask questions in plain language and get answers grounded in your own uploaded documents — with sessions you can revisit any time.

Session-based security

Team access always requires an active company session underneath it — so a team can never be reached independently of the company that owns it.

One dashboard, full visibility

See total teams, total documents, and recent activity at a glance the moment you log in — no digging required.

Built to scale with you

Add teams as your company grows. Each one gets the same document + chat tooling, scoped to exactly what it owns.
